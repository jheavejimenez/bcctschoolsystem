Mission

The Bulacan College of Commerce and Trade,Inc. (BCCT) is a
center of practical and relevant educational programs, centered on
top quality training and experiences. We equip future professionals
with the skills to fulfill local and global demands.


Vision

Our Students are armed with know-how and commitment to be the
best professionals they can be. They are competent, confident and
proficient in their chosen fields.

Our students are imbued with social awareness and integrity.
They understand that they are part of a global community
and are dedicated to the promotion of its progress
